Introduction

A Gantt chart is a powerful project management tool that provides a visual representation of project schedules, timelines, and tasks. It is named after its creator, Henry L. Gantt, and has become an indispensable tool for project managers, teams, and stakeholders across various industries. By displaying tasks and their dependencies along a timeline, Gantt charts offer a clear and organized way to plan, track, and manage projects efficiently. This use case explores how Gantt charts can enhance project management processes and help teams achieve their goals with greater precision and transparency
